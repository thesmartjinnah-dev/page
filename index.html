About Our

Journey of Excellence

Lt Safiullah Shaheed Academy was established in September 2010 by Lieutenant General Khalid Shameem Wayne as a welfare project for the wards of Shuhadas, Officers, JCOs, Soldiers, Defence Paid Employees & Civilians.

Our journey began with a mission to provide quality education at the most reasonable fee structure, operating on a "NO PROFIT NO LOSS BASIS". We are proud to have successfully conducted numerous sessions since our inception, nurturing academic excellence in the hearts of Quetta Garrison.

In Honor Of

Lt Safiullah Shaheed

‚≠ê Sitara-e-Basalat

Born on 19th January 1987 at Killi Meer Sharif of Nushki. Joined 117 L/C in Apr 2006. Commissioned in 16 Baloch Regiment in April 2008.

On 17th May 2009, while undertaking operations against Terrorists in Op Rah-e-Haq, Swat, the brave son of soil embraced Shahadat.

Lt Safiullah (Shaheed) Education Academy was established to acknowledge his services to the country.

Dear Students and Parents,

It is with great pride and honor that I welcome you to Lt Safiullah Shaheed Academy, an institution dedicated to nurturing academic excellence and character development.

At LSSA, we believe that education is not just about scoring high marks, but about developing critical thinking, moral values, and a passion for lifelong learning.
